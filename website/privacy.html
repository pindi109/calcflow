Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature. Here's exactly how CalcFlow handles your data.

Last updated: 1 January 2024

Summary: CalcFlow is a local-first calculator app. We collect minimal data, we never sell it, and you have full rights over whatever data we do hold. All calculations are performed and stored on your device.

1. Who We Are

CalcFlow ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller responsible for your personal data. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@calcflow.app
Address: CalcFlow Ltd, [Registered Address], England, United Kingdom

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

When you contact us or sign up for updates, we may collect:

  • Name and email address (if provided via contact forms)
  • Support enquiries and correspondence
  • Feedback and feature requests you voluntarily share

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

When you use the CalcFlow app, we may automatically collect limited technical data:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash reports and error logs (anonymised)
  • App launch frequency and session duration (aggregated, anonymised)
  • General geographic region (country level only, via IP address)

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your calculation history — this stays entirely on your device
  • Precise location data
  • Contact lists, camera, or microphone data
  • Any biometric data
  • Financial or payment information

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data only where we have a lawful basis under UK/EU GDPR. The purposes and legal bases are:

  • To provide and maintain the app — Legitimate interests (ensuring app functionality and security)
  • To respond to support enquiries — Legitimate interests and performance of a contract
  • To improve the app — Legitimate interests (analysing anonymised usage data to improve features)
  • To send updates (if opted in) — Consent
  • To comply with legal obligations — Legal obligation

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website (calcflow.app) uses the following cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled. No consent required.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website (via Google Analytics). These are only set with your consent.

The CalcFlow mobile app itself does not use cookies. Where we use analytics SDKs within the app, they are configured to anonymise data and respect user privacy settings at the platform level (iOS Privacy controls and Android Privacy Dashboard).

5. Third-Party Services

CalcFlow integrates with the following third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy which governs their data processing:

  • Google Firebase — Used for crash reporting (Crashlytics) and anonymised analytics. Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Data may be transferred to the US under Standard Contractual Clauses. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google Analytics — Used on our website to understand traffic patterns. IP anonymisation is enabled. Google Privacy Policy →

We only engage third parties who provide appropriate safeguards for personal data and comply with applicable data protection law.

6. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers are based outside the UK and EEA. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK ICO or European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Service providers certified under appropriate frameworks

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy:

  • Support correspondence: Retained for up to 2 years after resolution
  • Crash and error logs: Retained for up to 90 days in anonymised form
  • Analytics data: Retained in aggregated form for up to 26 months
  • Email list subscribers: Until you unsubscribe

8. Your Rights Under UK/EU GDPR (Articles 13 & 14)

As a data subject, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17): You can request deletion of your personal data where there is no compelling reason for continued processing.
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18): You can ask us to pause processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object (Art. 21): You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@calcflow.app. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.

9. Children's Privacy

CalcFlow is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@calcflow.app.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. These include encrypted data transmission (TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email (if you have provided one). The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of CalcFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, data subject access requests, or concerns, please contact:

Data Controller: CalcFlow Ltd
Email: privacy@calcflow.app
Website: calcflow.app/contact
Response time: Within 30 calendar days
